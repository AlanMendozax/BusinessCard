SICODE Project

Comprehensive detainee control C# system

Summary

Design the catalogs and database structure, as well as the graphic part for the information management system of alleged offenders for municipal public safety in Rocky Point Sonora, in order to optimize the security of the data and their handling.

Problem

The Rocky Point Township Department of Public Safety had a manual detainee registry. The information remained in physical documents that were in danger of being easily lost or destroyed when consulted or when a contingency occurred within the department.

Description

The technologies and methodologies implemented in this software are:

  • C#
  • Digital persona 4500 SDK
  • Layered architecture
  • SCRUM
  • SQL
  • POO
  • WinForm
